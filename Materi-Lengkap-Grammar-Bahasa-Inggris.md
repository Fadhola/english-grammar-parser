# BAGIAN 1 — PART OF SPEECH

**Part of speech** adalah penggolongan kata berdasarkan fungsinya di dalam kalimat. Dalam bahasa Inggris ada **10 jenis** utama. Memahami ini adalah fondasi dari semua tata bahasa — kalau bagian ini kuat, sisanya jauh lebih mudah.

> **Konsep penting:** satu kata yang sama bisa berperan sebagai jenis yang berbeda tergantung posisinya. Contoh: *book* bisa noun ("I read a **book**") atau verb ("I want to **book** a ticket"). Jadi jangan menghafal "kata X = noun", tapi pahami **fungsinya dalam kalimat**.

---

## 1.1 NOUN (Kata Benda)

**Definisi:** kata yang menyebut nama orang, tempat, benda, hewan, atau konsep/ide.

**Pertanyaan kunci untuk mengenali noun:** *"Apakah kata ini bisa didahului 'a/an/the' atau bisa dijamakkan?"*

### Jenis-jenis Noun

| Jenis | Penjelasan | Contoh |
|---|---|---|
| **Common noun** | Benda umum (huruf kecil) | *dog, city, teacher* |
| **Proper noun** | Nama khusus (huruf besar) | *Jakarta, Fadhola, Monday* |
| **Concrete noun** | Bisa diindra (dilihat/disentuh) | *table, water, music* |
| **Abstract noun** | Konsep yang tak bisa disentuh | *freedom, love, beauty* |
| **Countable noun** | Bisa dihitung | *book → books* |
| **Uncountable noun** | Tak bisa dihitung satuan | *water, information, advice* |
| **Collective noun** | Kelompok | *team, family, audience* |

### Abstract Noun yang Dibentuk dari Kata Lain (Word Formation)

Ini bagian yang Anda tanyakan — banyak abstract noun **diturunkan** dari verb atau adjective dengan menambahkan **sufiks (akhiran)**. Ini disebut *derivational morphology*.

**Dari VERB → NOUN:**

| Verb | Sufiks | Noun |
|---|---|---|
| decide | -sion | deci**sion** (keputusan) |
| inform | -ation | inform**ation** (informasi) |
| arrive | -al | arriv**al** (kedatangan) |
| move | -ment | move**ment** (gerakan) |
| fail | -ure | fail**ure** (kegagalan) |
| know | -ledge | know**ledge** (pengetahuan) |
| act | -ion | act**ion** (tindakan) |

**Dari ADJECTIVE → NOUN:**

| Adjective | Sufiks | Noun |
|---|---|---|
| happy | -ness | happi**ness** (kebahagiaan) |
| free | -dom | free**dom** (kebebasan) |
| able | -ity | abil**ity** (kemampuan) |
| warm | -th | warm**th** (kehangatan) |
| kind | -ness | kind**ness** (kebaikan) |
| different | -ence | differ**ence** (perbedaan) |
| brave | -ery | brav**ery** (keberanian) |

> **Tips morfologi:** sufiks pembentuk noun yang paling umum: **-tion/-sion, -ment, -ness, -ity, -ance/-ence, -ship, -dom, -hood, -age**. Kalau Anda lihat akhiran ini, hampir pasti kata itu noun.

**Contoh dalam kalimat:**
- *Her **kindness** surprised everyone.* (kindness = noun dari adjective "kind")
- *The **decision** was difficult.* (decision = noun dari verb "decide")
- *We value **freedom** and **honesty**.*

### Tips Membedakan Noun
- Bisa diberi **the/a/an**: "**the** decision" ✓
- Bisa menjadi **subjek** atau **objek** kalimat
- Sering diakhiri sufiks noun (-tion, -ness, -ment, dll.)

---

## 1.2 PRONOUN (Kata Ganti)

**Definisi:** kata yang menggantikan noun supaya tidak diulang-ulang.

> Tanpa pronoun: *"Fadhola opened Fadhola's laptop because Fadhola needed Fadhola's notes."*
> Dengan pronoun: *"Fadhola opened **his** laptop because **he** needed **his** notes."* — jauh lebih natural.

### Jenis-jenis Pronoun

| Jenis | Fungsi | Contoh |
|---|---|---|
| **Personal (subjek)** | Pelaku | I, you, he, she, it, we, they |
| **Personal (objek)** | Penerima aksi | me, you, him, her, it, us, them |
| **Possessive** | Kepemilikan | mine, yours, his, hers, ours, theirs |
| **Reflexive** | Aksi ke diri sendiri | myself, yourself, himself, themselves |
| **Demonstrative** | Menunjuk | this, that, these, those |
| **Interrogative** | Bertanya | who, whom, whose, which, what |
| **Relative** | Menghubungkan klausa | who, which, that, whom, whose |
| **Indefinite** | Tak tentu | someone, anything, everybody, none |

**Contoh dalam kalimat:**
- *Subjek:* ***She*** *works at a startup.*
- *Objek:* *The CEO interviewed* ***him***.
- *Possessive:* *This laptop is* ***mine***.
- *Reflexive:* *He taught* ***himself*** *Python.*
- *Demonstrative:* ***These*** *are my projects.*

### Tips Membedakan Pronoun vs Noun
- Pronoun **tidak menyebut nama spesifik** — ia hanya menunjuk.
- Hati-hati possessive: **its** (kepunyaan "it", tanpa apostrof) vs **it's** (= it is). Ini kesalahan paling sering.

---

## 1.3 VERB (Kata Kerja)

**Definisi:** kata yang menyatakan **aksi** (tindakan) atau **state** (keadaan/kondisi). Verb adalah "mesin" kalimat — hampir setiap kalimat butuh verb.

### Jenis-jenis Verb

| Jenis | Penjelasan | Contoh |
|---|---|---|
| **Action verb** | Tindakan nyata | run, write, eat, build |
| **Stative verb** | Keadaan/kondisi | know, believe, love, seem |
| **Linking verb** | Menghubungkan subjek ke deskripsinya | be (am/is/are), become, seem, feel |
| **Auxiliary (helping)** | Kata kerja bantu | be, do, have |
| **Modal** | Bantu khusus (kemungkinan, izin, dll.) | can, will, must, should |
| **Transitive** | Butuh objek | *She **wrote** a report.* |
| **Intransitive** | Tidak butuh objek | *He **slept**.* |

**Action vs Linking verb (penting!):**
- *Action:* *She **feels** the fabric.* (feel = menyentuh, ada aksi)
- *Linking:* *She **feels** tired.* (feel = keadaan, menghubungkan "she" ke "tired")

> **Trik:** kalau verb bisa diganti dengan tanda "=" maknanya tetap masuk akal, itu **linking verb**. *"She feels tired" → "She = tired"* ✓ berarti linking.

### Bentuk Verb (Verb Forms)

Setiap verb punya 5 bentuk. Ini penting untuk membentuk tenses:

| Base (V1) | Past (V2) | Past Participle (V3) | -ing (V-ing) | -s/es (V-s) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| go | went | gone | going | goes |
| write | wrote | written | writing | writes |
| build | built | built | building | builds |
| work | worked | worked | working | works |

> **Regular verbs** membentuk V2 & V3 dengan **-ed** (work→worked). **Irregular verbs** berubah tak beraturan (go→went→gone). Irregular harus dihafal.

### Tips Membedakan Verb
- Bisa diubah ke bentuk lampau: *work → worked*
- Bisa diberi *to* di depannya (infinitive): *to run, to think*

---

## 1.4 ADJECTIVE (Kata Sifat)

**Definisi:** kata yang **menjelaskan/menerangkan noun atau pronoun** — memberi tahu sifat, ukuran, warna, jumlah, kualitas.

**Pertanyaan kunci:** *"Kata ini menjawab pertanyaan: yang mana? seperti apa? berapa banyak?"*

**Posisi adjective:**
1. **Sebelum noun (attributive):** *a **beautiful** city*
2. **Setelah linking verb (predicative):** *The city is **beautiful**.*

**Contoh dalam kalimat:**
- *She built a **scalable** system.* (scalable menerangkan "system")
- *The interview was **difficult**.* (difficult menerangkan "interview")

### Tingkat Perbandingan (Degrees of Comparison)

| Positive | Comparative | Superlative |
|---|---|---|
| big | bigg**er** | the bigg**est** |
| smart | smart**er** | the smart**est** |
| beautiful | **more** beautiful | the **most** beautiful |
| good | **better** (irregular) | the **best** |
| bad | **worse** (irregular) | the **worst** |

> **Aturan:** kata pendek (1 suku kata) pakai **-er/-est**. Kata panjang (3+ suku kata) pakai **more/most**.

### Adjective Dibentuk dari Kata Lain

Seperti noun, banyak adjective diturunkan dengan sufiks:

| Asal | Sufiks | Adjective |
|---|---|---|
| beauty (noun) | -ful | beauti**ful** |
| care (noun) | -less | care**less** |
| nature (noun) | -al | natur**al** |
| create (verb) | -ive | creat**ive** |
| read (verb) | -able | read**able** |
| danger (noun) | -ous | danger**ous** |

> Sufiks adjective umum: **-ful, -less, -ous, -al, -ive, -able/-ible, -ic, -y, -ish**.

### Tips Membedakan Adjective
- Letaknya **sebelum noun** atau **setelah be/seem/become**.
- Bisa diberi "very": *very tall* ✓ (kalau "very + kata" masuk akal, biasanya adjective/adverb).

---

## 1.5 ADVERB (Kata Keterangan)

**Definisi:** kata yang **menerangkan verb, adjective, atau adverb lain**. Memberi tahu *bagaimana, kapan, di mana, seberapa sering, seberapa*.

**Pertanyaan kunci:** *how? when? where? how often? to what degree?*

### Jenis-jenis Adverb

| Jenis | Menjawab | Contoh |
|---|---|---|
| **Manner** (cara) | How? | quickly, carefully, well |
| **Time** (waktu) | When? | now, yesterday, soon, already |
| **Place** (tempat) | Where? | here, there, everywhere |
| **Frequency** (frekuensi) | How often? | always, often, never, sometimes |
| **Degree** (tingkat) | How much? | very, too, quite, almost |

**Contoh dalam kalimat:**
- *Menerangkan verb:* *She codes* ***efficiently***. (bagaimana dia coding?)
- *Menerangkan adjective:* *The task was* ***extremely*** *hard.* (seberapa hard?)
- *Menerangkan adverb lain:* *He runs* ***very*** *quickly.*

### Adjective vs Adverb — Perbedaan Tersulit

Banyak adverb dibentuk dari adjective + **-ly**:

| Adjective | Adverb |
|---|---|
| quick | quick**ly** |
| careful | careful**ly** |
| easy | eas**ily** |
| good | **well** (irregular!) |

**Cara membedakan:**
- **Adjective** menerangkan **noun**: *a **quick** runner* (quick → runner = noun)
- **Adverb** menerangkan **verb**: *He runs **quickly**.* (quickly → runs = verb)

> **Jebakan umum:** ❌ *"She sings good"* → ✓ *"She sings **well**"* (karena menerangkan verb "sings", butuh adverb).
> Tapi: ✓ *"She is a **good** singer"* (good menerangkan noun "singer").

> **Hati-hati:** tidak semua kata berakhiran -ly itu adverb! *friendly, lovely, lonely* adalah **adjective**.

---

## 1.6 PREPOSITION (Kata Depan)

**Definisi:** kata yang menunjukkan **hubungan** antara noun/pronoun dengan kata lain dalam kalimat — biasanya hubungan **tempat, waktu, arah, atau cara**.

### Kelompok Preposition

**Tempat (Place):** in, on, at, under, above, between, behind, beside, near
**Waktu (Time):** in, on, at, before, after, during, since, until
**Arah (Direction):** to, from, into, onto, through, across, toward

**Contoh dalam kalimat:**
- *The book is **on** the table.* (tempat)
- *We meet **at** 9 a.m.* (waktu)
- *She walked **into** the room.* (arah)

### Aturan in/on/at (paling membingungkan)

| | TIME | PLACE |
|---|---|---|
| **at** | jam: *at 9 a.m.* | titik spesifik: *at the door* |
| **on** | hari/tanggal: *on Monday* | permukaan: *on the wall* |
| **in** | bulan/tahun: *in 2026* | ruang/area: *in Jakarta* |

> **Logika:** **in** = besar/tertutup, **on** = permukaan/hari, **at** = titik spesifik. Dari besar ke kecil: in → on → at.

### Tips
- Preposition **selalu diikuti noun/pronoun** (disebut "object of preposition"): *in **the room**, to **him***.
- ❌ *"in he"* → ✓ *"in him"* (harus objek).

---

## 1.7 CONJUNCTION (Kata Penghubung)

**Definisi:** kata yang **menghubungkan** kata, frasa, atau klausa.

### 3 Jenis Conjunction

**1. Coordinating Conjunction** — menghubungkan dua hal **setara**. Ingat dengan **FANBOYS**:
> **F**or, **A**nd, **N**or, **B**ut, **O**r, **Y**et, **S**o

*I applied to BCA Digital,* ***and*** *I interviewed at Transjakarta.*

**2. Subordinating Conjunction** — menghubungkan klausa **tidak setara** (anak kalimat ke induk kalimat):
> because, although, when, if, while, since, unless, after, before, until

*He got the job* ***because*** *he prepared well.*

**3. Correlative Conjunction** — pasangan kata:
> both...and, either...or, neither...nor, not only...but also

***Both*** *the HR* ***and*** *the technical interview went well.*

### Tips Membedakan
- **Coordinating** (FANBOYS) menghubungkan dua kalimat **yang masing-masing bisa berdiri sendiri**.
- **Subordinating** membuat satu klausa **bergantung** pada yang lain.

---

## 1.8 INTERJECTION (Kata Seru)

**Definisi:** kata/ungkapan yang menyatakan **emosi atau reaksi spontan**. Biasanya berdiri sendiri dan diikuti tanda seru (!) atau koma.

**Contoh:** *Wow! Oh! Ouch! Hey! Hooray! Oops! Well, Alas, Hmm*

**Contoh dalam kalimat:**
- ***Wow!*** *I got the offer!*
- ***Oops****, I sent the wrong file.*
- ***Hey****, can you help me?*

### Tips
- Interjection **tidak punya hubungan tata bahasa** dengan kalimat lainnya — bisa dihapus tanpa merusak struktur kalimat.

---

## 1.9 DETERMINER (Kata Penentu)

**Definisi:** kata yang **mendahului noun** untuk menentukan/membatasi maknanya (yang mana, milik siapa, berapa).

### Jenis Determiner

| Jenis | Contoh |
|---|---|
| **Articles** | a, an, the |
| **Demonstratives** | this, that, these, those |
| **Possessives** | my, your, his, her, our, their |
| **Numbers** | one, two, first, second |

**Contoh dalam kalimat:**
- ***The*** *laptop is new.*
- ***My*** *project is finished.*
- ***This*** *book is interesting.*

### Articles: a / an / the

- **a/an** = umum, satu (belum tentu/baru disebut). **a** + bunyi konsonan (*a book*), **an** + bunyi vokal (*an apple, an hour*).
  > Yang dilihat **bunyi**, bukan huruf: *an **h**our* (h tak dibunyikan), *a **u**niversity* (dibaca "yu-").
- **the** = spesifik (sudah jelas/sudah disebut). *I bought **a** book. **The** book is good.*

### Determiner vs Adjective
- **Determiner** datang **lebih dulu** dan menentukan: *my, the, this*.
- **Adjective** datang setelah determiner dan **menggambarkan**: *the **big** house*.
- Urutan: **Determiner → Adjective → Noun** → *the big house*, *my new laptop*.

---

## 1.10 QUANTIFIER (Kata Kuantitas)

**Definisi:** sejenis determiner khusus yang menunjukkan **jumlah/kuantitas** noun. (Banyak buku menganggap quantifier sebagai bagian dari determiner.)

### Quantifier dan Pasangannya

| Quantifier | Dipakai dengan | Contoh |
|---|---|---|
| **many** | countable | *many books* |
| **much** | uncountable | *much water* |
| **a lot of / lots of** | keduanya | *a lot of friends / water* |
| **a few / few** | countable | *a few ideas* |
| **a little / little** | uncountable | *a little time* |
| **some** | keduanya (kalimat positif) | *some apples / some milk* |
| **any** | keduanya (negatif/tanya) | *any questions?* |
| **several** | countable | *several options* |

**Contoh dalam kalimat:**
- *She has **many** certificates.* (countable)
- *There isn't **much** time left.* (uncountable)
- *I need **a little** help.*

### Tips Membedakan many/much
- **many** + benda yang **bisa dihitung** (books, people, ideas)
- **much** + benda yang **tidak bisa dihitung** (water, time, information)
- Aman pakai **a lot of** kalau ragu (cocok untuk keduanya).

---

> ### 📌 Ringkasan Cepat Part of Speech
> | Jenis | Fungsi singkat | Contoh kata |
> |---|---|---|
> | Noun | nama benda/ide | dog, freedom |
> | Pronoun | pengganti noun | he, this, who |
> | Verb | aksi/keadaan | run, is, seem |
> | Adjective | menerangkan noun | happy, big |
> | Adverb | menerangkan verb/adj | quickly, very |
> | Preposition | hubungan ruang/waktu | in, on, to |
> | Conjunction | penghubung | and, but, because |
> | Interjection | seruan emosi | wow!, oops! |
> | Determiner | penentu noun | the, my, this |
> | Quantifier | jumlah noun | many, much, some |

# BAGIAN 2 — STRUKTUR KALIMAT

Sebelum masuk ke jenis kalimat, kita perlu memahami **klausa** secara singkat (dibahas lengkap di Bagian 4):
- **Independent clause** = klausa yang **bisa berdiri sendiri** sebagai kalimat (punya subjek + verb + makna utuh).
- **Dependent clause** = klausa yang **tidak bisa berdiri sendiri** (butuh klausa lain).

Empat jenis kalimat dibedakan berdasarkan **berapa banyak** dan **jenis** klausa yang dipakai.

---

## 2.1 SIMPLE SENTENCE (Kalimat Sederhana)

**Definisi:** satu **independent clause** saja. Punya satu subjek + satu predikat (verb), menyampaikan satu ide lengkap.

> Rumus: **1 Independent Clause**

**Contoh:**
- *Fadhola builds AI systems.*
  → Subjek: *Fadhola* | Verb: *builds* | Objek: *AI systems*
- *The interview went well.*
- *She and her team launched the product.*
  → ⚠️ Tetap simple sentence! Subjeknya **majemuk** (She and her team) tapi tetap **satu klausa**.

> **Catatan penting:** "simple" bukan berarti pendek. *"The talented young engineer from Jakarta confidently presented her RAG system to the panel of investors."* — panjang, tapi **tetap simple sentence** karena hanya satu klausa.

---

## 2.2 COMPOUND SENTENCE (Kalimat Majemuk Setara)

**Definisi:** dua atau lebih **independent clause** yang digabung. Masing-masing klausa **setara** (sama-sama bisa berdiri sendiri).

> Rumus: **Independent Clause + penghubung + Independent Clause**

**Cara menggabungkan:**

1. **Coordinating conjunction (FANBOYS)** + koma:
   *She prepared for the interview**, and** she got the job.*

2. **Semicolon (;)**:
   *She prepared for the interview**;** she got the job.*

3. **Conjunctive adverb** (however, therefore, moreover) + semicolon + koma:
   *She prepared well**; therefore,** she got the job.*

**Contoh:**
- *Fadhola applied to many companies**, but** he focused on AI roles.*
- *The HR round was easy**; however,** the technical round was hard.*
- *You can take the job offer**, or** you can keep searching.*

> **Tips:** kalau **kedua sisi penghubung bisa jadi kalimat utuh sendiri**, itu compound. Cek: "She prepared for the interview." ✓ utuh. "She got the job." ✓ utuh. → compound.

---

## 2.3 COMPLEX SENTENCE (Kalimat Kompleks / Majemuk Bertingkat)

**Definisi:** satu **independent clause** + satu atau lebih **dependent clause**. Ada hubungan **induk-anak** (tidak setara).

> Rumus: **Independent Clause + Dependent Clause**

Dependent clause biasanya diawali **subordinating conjunction** (because, although, when, if, since, while) atau **relative pronoun** (who, which, that).

**Contoh:**
- ***Because he prepared well****, he got the job.*
  → Dependent: *Because he prepared well* (tidak bisa berdiri sendiri)
  → Independent: *he got the job* (bisa berdiri sendiri)
- *He got the job* ***because he prepared well***. (urutan dibalik, makna sama)
- *The candidate* ***who built RAG systems*** *impressed the panel.*

> **Aturan koma:** kalau dependent clause **di depan**, beri koma. Kalau di belakang, biasanya tanpa koma.
> - *When I finished, I submitted it.* (depan → pakai koma)
> - *I submitted it when I finished.* (belakang → tanpa koma)

> **Tips membedakan compound vs complex:**
> - **Compound** = dua klausa **setara** (FANBOYS / ;) → keduanya bisa berdiri sendiri.
> - **Complex** = satu klausa **bergantung** pada yang lain (because, although, when...) → ada yang tak bisa berdiri sendiri.

---

## 2.4 COMPOUND-COMPLEX SENTENCE (Kalimat Majemuk-Kompleks)

**Definisi:** gabungan keduanya — **minimal dua independent clause** DAN **minimal satu dependent clause**.

> Rumus: **2+ Independent Clause + 1+ Dependent Clause**

**Contoh:**
- ***Because he prepared well****, he got the offer**, and** he started immediately.*

Mari kita bedah:
| Bagian | Jenis |
|---|---|
| *Because he prepared well* | Dependent clause |
| *he got the offer* | Independent clause 1 |
| *and he started immediately* | Independent clause 2 (dihubung "and") |

**Contoh lain:**
- *I submitted my CV* ***when the posting opened***, *but I haven't heard back yet.*
  → Dependent: *when the posting opened*
  → Independent 1: *I submitted my CV*
  → Independent 2: *but I haven't heard back yet*

---

> ### 📌 Ringkasan Struktur Kalimat
> | Jenis | Independent | Dependent | Penanda |
> |---|---|---|---|
> | **Simple** | 1 | 0 | satu ide |
> | **Compound** | 2+ | 0 | FANBOYS, ; |
> | **Complex** | 1 | 1+ | because, when, who... |
> | **Compound-Complex** | 2+ | 1+ | kombinasi keduanya |

# BAGIAN 3 — FRASA (PHRASE)

**Definisi frasa:** sekelompok kata yang berfungsi sebagai **satu unit**, tetapi **TIDAK memiliki kombinasi subjek + verb** (predikat). Inilah beda utama frasa vs klausa.

> **Frasa** = grup kata tanpa subjek-predikat lengkap → *"on the table", "very tired", "the tall building"*
> **Klausa** = grup kata DENGAN subjek + verb → *"because she was tired"*

Frasa dinamai sesuai **kata inti (head)**-nya.

---

## 3.1 NOUN PHRASE (Frasa Nomina)

**Inti:** noun. **Fungsi:** sebagai subjek, objek, atau pelengkap — sama seperti noun tunggal, tetapi lebih kaya.

**Struktur:** (Determiner) + (Adjective) + **NOUN** + (modifier)

**Contoh:**
- ***The talented engineer*** *got hired.* (sebagai subjek)
- *She built* ***a scalable document pipeline***. (sebagai objek)
- ***The candidate with production experience*** *stood out.* (noun + prepositional phrase)

> **Fungsi:** noun phrase bisa menempati **posisi mana pun** yang bisa ditempati noun.

---

## 3.2 VERB PHRASE (Frasa Verba)

**Inti:** main verb. **Fungsi:** sebagai predikat — menyatakan aksi/keadaan, sering bersama auxiliary verb.

**Struktur:** (Auxiliary/modal) + **MAIN VERB** + (objek/komplemen)

**Contoh:**
- *She* ***has been working*** *on the prototype.* (auxiliary: has been + main verb: working)
- *He* ***will submit*** *the report tomorrow.* (modal: will + verb: submit)
- *They* ***are building*** *an agent system.*

> **Fungsi:** menjadi "mesin aksi" kalimat. Verb phrase mencakup verb utama beserta semua kata bantu dan objeknya.

---

## 3.3 PREPOSITIONAL PHRASE (Frasa Preposisi)

**Inti:** preposition. **Struktur:** **PREPOSITION** + (determiner) + noun/pronoun (object of preposition).

**Fungsi ganda:** bisa berperan sebagai **adjective** (menerangkan noun) atau **adverb** (menerangkan verb).

**Contoh:**
- *The book* ***on the table*** *is mine.* → sebagai **adjective** (menerangkan "book": buku yang mana?)
- *She works* ***at a startup***. → sebagai **adverb** (menerangkan "works": di mana?)
- *We met* ***after the interview***. → sebagai **adverb** (kapan?)

> **Ciri:** selalu dimulai preposition dan diakhiri noun/pronoun. Tidak pernah mengandung subjek + verb.

---

## 3.4 ADJECTIVE PHRASE (Frasa Adjektiva)

**Inti:** adjective. **Fungsi:** menerangkan noun/pronoun, sama seperti adjective tunggal tapi diperluas (sering dengan adverb atau prepositional phrase).

**Contoh:**
- *The system is* ***incredibly efficient***. (adverb "incredibly" + adjective "efficient")
- *She is* ***good at problem-solving***. (adjective "good" + prepositional phrase)
- *A* ***surprisingly difficult*** *question came up.*

> **Fungsi:** menggambarkan kualitas noun secara lebih detail. Cek: apakah keseluruhan frasa menjawab "seperti apa?" → ya, berarti adjective phrase.

---

## 3.5 ADVERBIAL PHRASE (Frasa Adverbia)

**Inti:** adverb. **Fungsi:** menerangkan verb/adjective/adverb — menjawab *bagaimana, kapan, di mana, seberapa sering*.

**Contoh:**
- *She finished the task* ***very quickly***. (adverb "very" + adverb "quickly" → bagaimana?)
- *He arrived* ***earlier than expected***. (kapan?)
- *They worked* ***quite efficiently***. (bagaimana?)

> **Catatan:** prepositional phrase juga bisa berfungsi sebagai adverbial (*"in the morning", "at the office"*). Jadi "adverbial phrase" sering dipakai luas untuk **frasa apa pun yang berperan seperti adverb**.

---

> ### 📌 Ringkasan Frasa
> | Frasa | Inti | Fungsi | Contoh |
> |---|---|---|---|
> | Noun | noun | subjek/objek | *the tall building* |
> | Verb | verb | predikat | *has been working* |
> | Prepositional | preposition | adj/adverb | *on the table* |
> | Adjective | adjective | menerangkan noun | *very tired* |
> | Adverbial | adverb | menerangkan verb | *quite slowly* |

---

# BAGIAN 4 — KLAUSA (CLAUSE)

**Definisi klausa:** sekelompok kata yang **memiliki subjek DAN verb**. Inilah pembeda utama dari frasa.

> Klausa **selalu** punya pasangan **subjek + verb**.
> *"she works"* → klausa ✓ | *"in the office"* → frasa (tidak ada verb)

---

## 4.1 INDEPENDENT CLAUSE (Klausa Bebas/Utama)

**Definisi:** klausa yang **bisa berdiri sendiri** sebagai kalimat lengkap. Mengandung subjek + verb + makna utuh.

**Contoh:**
- *Fadhola got the job.* ✓ (utuh, bisa jadi kalimat)
- *She built a RAG system.* ✓
- *The team launched the product.* ✓

> Setiap **simple sentence** sebenarnya adalah satu independent clause.

---

## 4.2 DEPENDENT CLAUSE (Klausa Terikat/Anak)

**Definisi:** klausa yang punya subjek + verb, tetapi **TIDAK bisa berdiri sendiri** karena diawali kata penghubung yang membuatnya "menggantung". Ia butuh independent clause untuk melengkapinya.

**Penanda dependent clause:**
- Subordinating conjunction: *because, although, when, if, since, while, after, before, unless*
- Relative pronoun: *who, which, that, whom, whose*

**Contoh:**
- *Because he prepared well...* ❌ (menggantung — "kenapa? lalu apa?")
- *...who built RAG systems...* ❌ (belum lengkap)
- *When the interview ended...* ❌

> Perhatikan: *"he prepared well"* bisa berdiri sendiri. Tapi begitu ditambah *"because"* di depannya → *"because he prepared well"* langsung menggantung. Kata penghubung itulah yang "mengikat" klausa.

### 3 Jenis Dependent Clause

| Jenis | Fungsi | Contoh |
|---|---|---|
| **Noun clause** | sebagai noun (subjek/objek) | *I know **that he passed**.* |
| **Adjective (relative) clause** | menerangkan noun | *The man **who called** is here.* |
| **Adverbial clause** | menerangkan verb (kapan/kenapa) | *I left **when it ended**.* |

---

## 4.3 Perbedaan & Hubungan Independent vs Dependent

| | Independent Clause | Dependent Clause |
|---|---|---|
| Subjek + Verb? | Ada ✓ | Ada ✓ |
| Bisa berdiri sendiri? | **Ya** | **Tidak** |
| Diawali penghubung? | Tidak harus | Hampir selalu |
| Contoh | *She got the job.* | *because she prepared.* |

**Hubungan keduanya:** dependent clause "menempel" pada independent clause untuk membentuk kalimat yang lebih kaya. Independent clause adalah **induk**, dependent clause adalah **anak** yang memberi konteks tambahan (alasan, waktu, syarat, deskripsi).

---

## 4.4 Contoh Penggabungan Klausa

**Independent + Independent (= Compound):**
> *She prepared well**, and** she got the job.*
> (dua klausa setara, dihubung "and")

**Independent + Dependent (= Complex):**
> *She got the job* ***because she prepared well***.
> (induk: "She got the job" + anak: "because she prepared well")

**Independent + Independent + Dependent (= Compound-Complex):**
> ***Although the test was hard***, *she passed it**, and** she felt proud.*
> (anak: "Although the test was hard" + induk 1: "she passed it" + induk 2: "she felt proud")

> ### 📌 Frasa vs Klausa — Pembeda Kunci
> | | Subjek + Verb? | Contoh |
> |---|---|---|
> | **Frasa** | TIDAK | *in the office, very tired* |
> | **Klausa** | YA | *she works, because she was tired* |

# BAGIAN 5 — TATA BAHASA (GRAMMAR)

---

## 5.1 TENSES (Bentuk Waktu)

Tense menunjukkan **kapan** suatu aksi terjadi (waktu) dan **bagaimana** keadaannya (aspek). Ada **3 waktu** (Present, Past, Future) × **4 aspek** (Simple, Continuous, Perfect, Perfect Continuous) = **12 tenses**.

Kita pakai contoh verb **work** (subjek: I/she).

### A. PRESENT (Sekarang)

**1. Simple Present** — kebiasaan, fakta umum, kebenaran.
- Rumus: **S + V1 (+s/es untuk he/she/it)**
- *She **works** at a startup.* | *I **work** every day.*
- Penanda: *always, usually, every day, never*

**2. Present Continuous** — sedang berlangsung sekarang.
- Rumus: **S + am/is/are + V-ing**
- *She **is working** on a prototype right now.*
- Penanda: *now, at the moment, currently*

**3. Present Perfect** — aksi lampau yang masih relevan sekarang / pengalaman.
- Rumus: **S + have/has + V3**
- *She **has finished** the report.* | *I **have built** three projects.*
- Penanda: *already, just, yet, ever, never, since, for*

**4. Present Perfect Continuous** — aksi yang dimulai di masa lampau dan masih berlangsung, menekankan durasi.
- Rumus: **S + have/has + been + V-ing**
- *She **has been working** here for six months.*
- Penanda: *for, since, how long*

### B. PAST (Lampau)

**5. Simple Past** — aksi selesai di masa lampau.
- Rumus: **S + V2**
- *She **worked** at Jatis Mobile.* | *I **built** a RAG system.*
- Penanda: *yesterday, last week, in 2025, ago*

**6. Past Continuous** — sedang berlangsung di titik tertentu masa lampau.
- Rumus: **S + was/were + V-ing**
- *She **was working** when the call came.*
- Penanda: *while, when, at that moment*

**7. Past Perfect** — aksi yang selesai **sebelum** aksi lampau lain.
- Rumus: **S + had + V3**
- *She **had finished** the task before the meeting started.*
- Penanda: *before, after, by the time, already*

**8. Past Perfect Continuous** — durasi aksi sebelum titik lampau lain.
- Rumus: **S + had + been + V-ing**
- *She **had been working** for hours before she took a break.*

### C. FUTURE (Akan Datang)

**9. Simple Future** — akan terjadi.
- Rumus: **S + will + V1**
- *She **will start** next Monday.*
- Penanda: *tomorrow, next week, soon*

**10. Future Continuous** — akan sedang berlangsung di titik tertentu nanti.
- Rumus: **S + will + be + V-ing**
- *This time tomorrow, she **will be working**.*

**11. Future Perfect** — akan selesai sebelum titik tertentu nanti.
- Rumus: **S + will + have + V3**
- *By next year, she **will have completed** the project.*

**12. Future Perfect Continuous** — durasi sampai titik tertentu nanti.
- Rumus: **S + will + have + been + V-ing**
- *By June, she **will have been working** here for a year.*

> ### 📌 Tabel Ringkas 12 Tenses
> | Aspek | Present | Past | Future |
> |---|---|---|---|
> | **Simple** | works | worked | will work |
> | **Continuous** | is working | was working | will be working |
> | **Perfect** | has worked | had worked | will have worked |
> | **Perfect Cont.** | has been working | had been working | will have been working |

---

## 5.2 SUBJECT-VERB AGREEMENT (Kesesuaian Subjek-Verb)

**Aturan inti:** verb harus **sesuai** dengan subjek dalam hal jumlah (tunggal/jamak).

**Aturan dasar:**
- Subjek **tunggal** (he/she/it) → verb **+s/es** (present): *She **works**.*
- Subjek **jamak** (they/we) → verb **tanpa s**: *They **work**.*

**Kasus rumit yang sering salah:**

| Situasi | Aturan | Contoh |
|---|---|---|
| **Each / every / everyone** | dianggap **tunggal** | *Everyone **is** here.* |
| **Subjek + "of"** | ikut kata sebelum "of" | *Each of the students **is** ready.* |
| **Two subjects + "and"** | **jamak** | *Tom and Jane **are** here.* |
| **"either...or"** | ikut subjek **terdekat** | *Either he or they **are** wrong.* |
| **Kata sisipan (with, as well as)** | abaikan, ikut subjek utama | *The manager, with his team, **is** here.* |
| **Uncountable noun** | **tunggal** | *The information **is** correct.* |
| **Collective noun** | biasanya tunggal | *The team **is** winning.* |

> **Jebakan klasik:** ❌ *"The list of items are long"* → ✓ *"The list of items **is** long"* (subjeknya "list", bukan "items").

---

## 5.3 PRONOUN AGREEMENT (Kesesuaian Pronoun)

**Aturan inti:** pronoun harus **cocok** dengan kata yang diacunya (antecedent) dalam **jumlah, gender, dan jenis (subjek/objek)**.

**Contoh benar:**
- *Fadhola finished **his** project.* (Fadhola = laki-laki tunggal → his)
- *The students submitted **their** reports.* (students = jamak → their)

**Kesalahan umum:**
- ❌ *"Each student must bring **their** book"* (formal: salah, karena "each" tunggal)
- ✓ *"Each student must bring **his or her** book"* (formal benar)
- ✓ *"**They**" sebagai singular* kini diterima luas dalam bahasa modern/informal.

**Subjek vs objek pronoun:**
- ✓ *Subjek:* ***She*** *and I went.* (bukan "Her and me went")
- ✓ *Objek:* *They invited **her** and **me**.* (bukan "she and I")
> **Trik:** hapus pronoun lain dan cek satu-satu. *"They invited **me**"* ✓ (bukan "they invited I").

---

## 5.4 MODAL VERBS (Kata Kerja Bantu Modal)

**Definisi:** verb bantu khusus yang menyatakan **kemungkinan, kemampuan, izin, kewajiban, saran**. Selalu diikuti **V1 (base form)** tanpa "to".

> Rumus: **S + modal + V1**

| Modal | Makna | Contoh |
|---|---|---|
| **can** | kemampuan/izin | *She **can** code in Python.* |
| **could** | kemampuan lampau/sopan | *Could you help me?* |
| **may** | izin/kemungkinan | *You **may** leave.* |
| **might** | kemungkinan kecil | *It **might** rain.* |
| **must** | kewajiban kuat/kepastian | *You **must** submit it.* |
| **should** | saran | *You **should** rest.* |
| **will** | masa depan/janji | *I **will** call you.* |
| **would** | pengandaian/sopan | *I **would** love to.* |
| **shall** | saran/penawaran (formal) | *Shall we begin?* |

> **Aturan penting:** ❌ *"She can to code"* / *"She cans code"* → ✓ *"She **can code**"*. Modal **tidak** pakai -s dan **tidak** diikuti "to".

---

## 5.5 ACTIVE & PASSIVE VOICE (Kalimat Aktif & Pasif)

**Active voice:** subjek **melakukan** aksi. (Fokus pada pelaku.)
**Passive voice:** subjek **dikenai** aksi. (Fokus pada penerima/hasil.)

> Rumus passive: **S + be + V3 (+ by pelaku)**

**Contoh transformasi:**
| Active | Passive |
|---|---|
| *She **wrote** the report.* | *The report **was written** by her.* |
| *They **build** systems.* | *Systems **are built** by them.* |
| *The team **will launch** it.* | *It **will be launched** by the team.* |

**Passive di berbagai tense:**
| Tense | Passive |
|---|---|
| Simple Present | is/are + V3 |
| Simple Past | was/were + V3 |
| Present Perfect | has/have been + V3 |
| Future | will be + V3 |
| Modal | modal + be + V3 (*must be done*) |

**Kapan pakai passive?**
- Pelaku **tidak penting/tidak diketahui**: *The window **was broken**.* (siapa? tidak penting)
- Fokus pada **hasil**: *The bridge **was completed** in 2024.*
- Konteks **formal/ilmiah**: *The data **was analyzed** using Python.*

---

## 5.6 CONDITIONAL SENTENCES (Kalimat Pengandaian)

**Definisi:** kalimat "if" yang menyatakan **syarat dan hasil**. Ada 4 tipe utama.

### Type 0 — Fakta/Kebenaran Umum
> **If + Simple Present, ... Simple Present**
- *If you **heat** water, it **boils**.* (selalu benar)

### Type 1 — Kemungkinan Nyata (masa depan)
> **If + Simple Present, ... will + V1**
- *If you **prepare** well, you **will pass** the interview.* (mungkin terjadi)

### Type 2 — Tidak Nyata Sekarang (hipotetis)
> **If + Simple Past, ... would + V1**
- *If I **had** more time, I **would build** another project.* (kenyataannya tidak punya waktu)
- > Catatan: pakai *"were"* untuk semua subjek: *If I **were** you...*

### Type 3 — Penyesalan Masa Lampau (tidak bisa diubah)
> **If + Past Perfect, ... would have + V3**
- *If I **had studied**, I **would have passed**.* (kenyataannya tidak belajar & tidak lulus)

> ### 📌 Ringkasan Conditional
> | Type | If-clause | Main clause | Makna |
> |---|---|---|---|
> | 0 | present | present | fakta umum |
> | 1 | present | will + V1 | nyata, mungkin |
> | 2 | past | would + V1 | hipotetis sekarang |
> | 3 | past perfect | would have + V3 | penyesalan lampau |

---

## 5.7 DIRECT & INDIRECT SPEECH (Kalimat Langsung & Tidak Langsung)

**Direct speech:** mengutip kata-kata **persis** seperti diucapkan, dalam tanda kutip.
**Indirect speech (reported):** melaporkan ucapan **tanpa tanda kutip**, dengan penyesuaian.

**Contoh dasar:**
- Direct: *She said, **"I am tired."***
- Indirect: *She said **(that) she was tired**.*

### Perubahan yang Terjadi

**1. Pergeseran Tense (mundur satu langkah):**
| Direct | Indirect |
|---|---|
| present → | past |
| *"I **work**"* | *...she **worked*** |
| *"I **am** working"* | *...she **was** working* |
| *"I **have** finished"* | *...she **had** finished* |
| *"I **will** go"* | *...she **would** go* |

**2. Perubahan Pronoun:**
- *"**I** like **my** job"* → *She said **she** liked **her** job.*

**3. Perubahan Keterangan Waktu/Tempat:**
| Direct | Indirect |
|---|---|
| now | then |
| today | that day |
| tomorrow | the next day |
| yesterday | the day before |
| here | there |
| this | that |

**Contoh lengkap:**
- Direct: *He said, **"I will finish this report tomorrow."***
- Indirect: *He said **(that) he would finish that report the next day**.*

### Pertanyaan & Perintah dalam Indirect Speech

**Pertanyaan (gunakan "asked", balik ke urutan kalimat biasa):**
- Direct: *She asked, **"Where do you work?"***
- Indirect: *She asked **where I worked**.* (tanpa "do", urutan normal)

- Yes/No question → pakai **if/whether**:
- Direct: *"Are you ready?"* → Indirect: *She asked **if I was ready**.*

**Perintah (gunakan "told/asked" + to + V1):**
- Direct: *"Submit the report."* → Indirect: *She told me **to submit the report**.*
- Larangan: *"Don't be late."* → *She told me **not to be late**.*
